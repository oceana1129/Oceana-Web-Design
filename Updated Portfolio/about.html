My name is Oceana

This is My Story

I am at heart a problem solver always seeking out my next challenge. And my journey in web development starts with my first business venture.

My dream was to sell and make a living off of my art. So I began to build an audience, creating merchandise, and working with clients. But as my ambitions grew ever bigger, I found myself needing a more efficient way to work with clients and increase my profits.

My solution? A website.

Coming to this conclusion, I set aside my drawing tools and buried my nose on any and every book I could find on web development. As time passed, I realized that I was spending more time emmersed in programming instead of drawing. And now here I am, working as a software developer - hoping to help others with their bring their dreams into fruition.
